# Food Delivery Application Using Drone Technology

A smart food delivery platform that leverages **autonomous drone technology** to provide faster, contactless, and eco-friendly deliveries. The system enables users to browse restaurants, place orders, track deliveries in real time, and receive food through GPS-enabled drones.

##  Features

*  Autonomous drone-based food delivery
*  User-friendly mobile application
*  Real-time GPS tracking
*  Secure online payments
*  Optimized delivery routes
*  Eco-friendly and contactless delivery

## Tech Stack

* Flutter (UI)
* Figma (UI/UX Design)
* GPS & Drone Navigation
* Real-Time Tracking
* Payment Integration

## Objective

To reduce delivery time, traffic dependency, and operational costs by integrating autonomous drones into the food delivery process while improving customer experience.

## Future Enhancements

* AI-powered route optimization
* Multi-drone fleet management
* Extended delivery range
* Advanced obstacle avoidance
* Smart charging stations
